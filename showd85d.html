      SÃO MARTINHO: ONE OF BRAZIL’S MOST INNOVATIVE COMPANIES

       

      São Paulo, July 27, 2022 – A São Martinho is among the 150 most innovative companies in Brazil for the seventh time in a row. The 8th edition of “Valor Inovação Brasil Award”, organized by Valor Econômico newspaper in partnership with consulting firm Strategy& and ANPEI, disclosed its annual ranking at an online ceremony – the Company ranked 61st in the national ranking.

      In the agribusiness sector, São Martinho remained in a prominent position, ranking 4th among the five best and most innovative companies. This is the 6th edition of the award since 2016 in which the Company ensures its place among the industry’s Top 5.

      With the topic “5G and the revolution in business models,” this year’s survey revealed the companies with the best innovation management and their investments in the local market. Based on qualitative and quantitative indicators, five pillars in the innovation chain were assessed: intention to innovate, effort to make the innovation happen, results obtained, market assessment and knowledge generation.

      “We believe that innovation creates sustainable value for our Company. In the past years, we have been following our digital transformation journey, turning São Martinho into one of the biggest smart farming cases in Brazil, making the first tests with the 5G network in the agribusiness sector. We seek to develop business and solutions, fostering open innovation and increasingly connecting our Company to the best players in the ecosystem,” states Agenor Pavan, COO and vice-president of São Martinho.

      For more information on the 8th edition of “Valor Inovação Brasil Award”, visit: premioinovacao2022.com.br
