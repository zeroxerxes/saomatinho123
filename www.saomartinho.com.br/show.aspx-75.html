      Privacy policy

      This Policy was drafted to demonstrate São Martinho's commitment to the security and privacy of data collected from Users while using the interactive services available here and may be revised any time without prior notice.

      DATA USED

      São Martinho may collect the information entered by Users at the time of registration, through data input in the forms: name, gender, profession, address and others; and also information collected automatically in a strictly compatible and on a need-to-know basis, when using the page and the network, such as the characteristics of access devices, IP identification with date and time of connection, cookies and caches when enabled, among others.

      USE

      Information is collected by São Martinho for the purpose of establishing a contractual relationship, disclosure of events or the management, administration, provision, expansion and improvement of the website for Users, in accordance with item I of article 7 of Federal Law 13,709/2018, also known as Brazil’s General Data Protection Law (“GDPL”).

      COOKIES

      São Martinho uses cookies, which are text files that can be stored on electronic devices when the Page is visited, for the following purposes: i) need (to ensure the functioning of the website); ii) performance (to understand the interaction of visitors with the website); iii) functionality (for the usage history to provide a personalized experience); iv) marketing; and these can be disabled according to the users’ preferences.

      Users may at any time revoke their consent regarding cookies by deleting them from the Page using the settings of their preferred browser, which could affect some features of the Page..

      SHARING

      São Martinho may share the information collected through the Page in the following cases: with government agencies, functional benefit consultants and administrators, health operators, financial institutions, business partners, service and/or infrastructure providers, among others, for the purposes mentioned earlier.

      Information collected by São Martinho will be automatically deleted from its servers when it is no longer useful for the purposes for which it was collected or when the User requests deletion of their personal data.

      Without prejudice, information may be stored for compliance with legal or regulatory obligations, or for transfer to third parties, provided the data processing requirements are respected - and for the exclusive use of São Martinho, with its access by third parties prohibited, provided it is kept anonymous.

      RIGHTS OF DATA OWNERS

      São Martinho will respect the rights of Data Owners established in the GDPL while processing the information.

      SAFETY

      São Martinho will store the information collected on its own servers or those hired by it, taking the best efforts and reasonable market measures, as well as those required legally to ensure the privacy of the data collected.

      Other organizations hired to provide support services will be required to comply with São Martinho's privacy standards.

      CONTACT

      In case of doubts related to this Privacy Policy or to apply your rights, please contact the “Person in Charge” for handling personal data at São Martinho by sending an email to: privacidade@saomartinho.com.br. Or visit our Privacy Channel here.

      LEGISLATION

      This Policy will be governed, interpreted and implemented in accordance with the Laws of the Federative Republic of Brazil, especially Federal Law 13,709/2018 (GDPL)
