      • Ethanol

      São Martinho also produces hydrated ethanol, used in tanks of cars powered by ethanol; anhydrous ethanol, which is mixed with gasoline as an additive to fuel the tanks of gasoline-powered vehicles; and industrial ethanol, mainly used in the production of paints, cosmetics and alcoholic beverages

      AGRICULTURAL PRODUCTION

      Soil preparation and correction, Planting, Fertilization, Fertigation, Pest Control, diseases and weeds, are some of the several operations that make up the cane production process, making available in quantity and quality suitable for harvesting

      HARVEST

      Our sugarcane fields are harvested through a mechanized process, using leading edge technologies such as high performance machines, high precision GPS, 4G communication, among others.

      TRANSPORT

      Then, high-capacity trucks transport the cane from the cane fields to the mills

      MILLING

      In the mills, the sugar cane is milled for the extraction of the broth, also used in the manufacture of sugar.

      TREATMENT

      The broth then undergoes heating, chemical treatment, decantation and sieving to remove impurities.

      FERMENTATION

      The pure broth receives the addition of yeasts responsible for its fermentation, producing ethanol and carbonic gas. The result is the fermented wine.

      CENTRIFUGATION

      The wine is subjected to the centrifugation process, which separates the yeast into the next fermentation cycle, leaving only ethanol and water for distillation.

      DISTILLATION

      Through evaporation and condensation, the ethanol is separated from the wine. This process results in hydrated ethanol, used as fuel.

      DEHYDRATION

      The hydrated ethanol goes through the dehydration process, in which the water is eliminated to obtain anhydrous ethanol, which is mixed with gasoline
