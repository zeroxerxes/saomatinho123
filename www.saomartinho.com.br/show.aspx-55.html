      Ethics & Compliance

      Throughout decades of work, growth, innovation and deep relationships with the workforce, partners, community and other stakeholders, São Martinho has created a history of tradition, culture and respect.

      Its reputation as a benchmark company in the national sugar and alcohol market is sustained by solid values and virtues that guide the professional conduct of all its employees. Fairness, honesty, impartiality, integrity, loyalty, trust, team spirit, solidarity, respect, transparency, discretion and common sense are some examples of these initiatives that must be practiced daily between co-workers, suppliers, customers, competitors, shareholders, government, family and society in general.

      São Martinho believes that the commitment and attitudes of its employees are key to guaranteeing the image of a company that is ethical, transparent and deeply cares about and respects people.
