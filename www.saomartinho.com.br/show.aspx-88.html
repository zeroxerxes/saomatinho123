      IRACEMA MILL RECEIVES ISO 14001 CERTIFICATION

      Mill is certified after implementing an Environmental Management System in its agroindustrial and administrative areas and conducting an independent audit
       

      São Paulo, January 27, 2022 - The Iracema Mill, located in Iracemápolis, São Paulo, was certified by ISO 14001, an international standard that set the criteria for implementing an Environmental Management System (EMS) devised for the unit, which established various operational practices, especially to ensure compliance with environmental legislation.

      Drawing on the involvement of employees from all areas of the company (agricultural, industrial and administrative), the unit underwent a lengthy diagnosis and audit process, from October 2020 to October 2021, which evaluated compliance with the standard’s requirements. These include, for example, creating management systematics for environmental impacts; controlling and assessing compliance with environmental legislation; mapping risks; optimizing processes for responsibly using, protecting and avoiding natural resources, among others.

      Training programs were also organized to ensure that employees were even better prepared to perform their functions.

      After conducting the diagnosis, implementing the EMS and completing the certification audit, which was performed by the Carlos Alberto Vanzolini Foundation (FCAV), the unit obtained the certification, which attests to the proper environmental management of its operations and production processes, as well as its commitment to the environment.

      Attaining ISO 14001 demonstrates São Martinho’s respect for the environment, environmental legislation and the requirements of international standards. Requiring continuous improvement, the certification will ensure that the company continues to meet the market’s ever-higher expectations in terms of responsible care and corporate governance.

      Today, in addition to the Iracema Mill, the Santa Cruz Mill also holds ISO 14001 certification. The company expects to expand soon its implementation of EMSs in accordance with the 14001 standard to its other units, the São Martinho and Boa Vista mills.
