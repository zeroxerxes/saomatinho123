      SÃO MARTINHO REPORTS CASH NET INCOME OF R$ 219.0 MILLION
      IN 4Q22, DOWN 4.2%

      Company announces to the market its financial results
      for the 4th quarter of 2021/22 crop year

       

      São Paulo, June 20, 2022 - São Martinho has announced the results of the fourth quarter and the overall results of the 2021/22 crop year. Agricultural yield and TRS produced decreased significantly during the crop year. In this context, the company adjusted its production mix, which enabled it to achieve healthy results.  

      In 4Q22, Adjusted EBITDA totaled R$ 770.8 million, increasing 35.6%, with Adjusted EBITDA margin of 51.9%. In the crop year, Adjusted EBITDA totaled R$ 3,141.9 million (+43.6%), with margin of 54.5%, mainly reflecting the higher average selling prices of ethanol and sugar.

      Net Income in 4Q22 was R$ 225.4 million, increasing 8.7% from 4Q21. In 12M22, net income totaled R$ 1,480.9 million, up 59.7%. Cash net income totaled R$ 219.0 million in the quarter, down 4.2% from 4Q21, while cash net income in the crop year totaled R$ 1,528.6 million, increasing 53.4% from the previous year.

      Another important financial indicator for the Company - the Leverage Ratio - ended the crop year at 0.93x Net Debt/Adjusted EBITDA on March 31, 2022, down 25.1% from March 31, 2021.
