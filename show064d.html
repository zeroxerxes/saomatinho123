      SÃO MARTINHO AMONG THE 10 BEST COMPANIES
      IN THE BROADCAST EMPRESAS RANKING

      The Company stood out for its financial performance and return to shareholders in 2021

       

      São Paulo, June 30, 2022 - São Martinho was one of the 10 Brazilian companies honored in the 22nd edition of “Prêmio Broadcast Empresas” [Broadcast Companies Award], held by Agência Estado in partnership with FGV.

      The annual ranking presented the 10 companies listed on B3 with the best financial performance and return to shareholders in 2021, which stood out in various criteria and indicators, such as variation in equity profitability; payment of dividends in relation to equity; price/earnings ratio (PE); price-to-book value (P/BV); stock fluctuation; stock volatility; and liquidity.

      In addition to São Martinho, the following companies were also awarded: WEG, RaiaDrogasil, Klabin, JBS, Braskem, Vale, Rede d'Or, Ambev, and Arezzo, among 204 companies assessed in the market.

      According to Fabio Venturelli, CEO of São Martinho, being among the 10 outstanding companies is a great honor for the Company and a very important recognition of the care taken with the management of the business. “I share this recognition with the entire company, and our more than 12,000 employees, who together make a difference,” he said.
