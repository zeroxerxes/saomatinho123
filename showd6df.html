      SÃO MARTINHO REPORTS NET INCOME OF R$ 696.9 MILLION IN 3Q22, UP 156.1%

      Company announces to the market its financial results for the 3rd quarter
      of the 2021/22 crop year

       

      São Paulo, February 14, 2022 - At the end of one more quarter of the 2021/22 crop year, São Martinho has reported healthy performance in its key financial indicators, with the highlight being net income of R$696.9 million in 3Q22, increasing 156.1% from the same period the previous crop year.

      Net revenue in the period was around R$ 1.53 billion, up 26.2% from 3Q21. Fueled by better average sale prices of ethanol (+76.9%) and sugar (+30.9%) in the market, adjusted EBITDA totaled R$892.8 million, 37% higher than in the previous year. EBITDA margin stood at 58.3% in 3Q22.

      From the operational viewpoint, São Martinho has already concluded its crushing period for the 2021/22 crop year, processing 19.9 million tons of sugarcane in 9M22, 11.6% less than in the same period the previous crop year, mainly due to the prolonged drought in the period and fewer harvest days than in 9M21.

      During the current crop year, the company produced around 1.3 million tons of sugar, down 12.1% from the 2020/21 crop year, as well as 913,000 m³ of ethanol (-10.4%) and 760,000 MWh of energy exported (-8.9%). The sugar/ethanol production mix remained stable year on year at 47%-53%. The TRS produced in the first nine months of the crop year was 11% lower than in the same period the previous crop year.

      Finally, the company's leverage ratio (Net Debt/Adjusted EBITDA) at the end of 3Q21 was 1.15x, about 10% lower than on December 31, 2020.
