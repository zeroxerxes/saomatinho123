      SÃO MARTINHO RAISES R$ 1.098 BILLION THROUGH INCENTIVIZED DEBENTURES
      FOR GREEN PROJECTS

       

      São Paulo, April 4, 2022 - São Martinho, one of Brazil’s largest sugar and ethanol groups, has recently announced that it raised R$1.098 billion through a public offering of incentivized debentures to finance investments in the production and storage of biofuels and biomass in its units.

      The issue received the Green Seal from S&P Global Ratings and certifies its alignment with the Green Bond Principles (GBP) of the International Capital Market Association (ICMA), which underscores the transparent use of funds and the focus on sustainable actions.

      XP was the sole lead manager of the debenture offering.
